How is this calculated?

The battle prediction uses a weighted scoring system that considers:

  • Survival Score = (HP × 0.35) + (Physical Defense × 0.25) + (Magic Defense × 0.25) + (HP Regen × 0.15)
  • Attack Score = (Physical Attack × 0.4) + (Attack Speed × 0.3) + (Skill Effectiveness × 0.3)
  • Utility Score = (Movement Speed × 0.4) + (Mana Regen × 0.3) + (Win Rate × 0.3)
